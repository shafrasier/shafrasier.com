the Clearing

the Clearing · session one

five biomes

July 5th, 2026.

selected by Sha Frasier and David Feigelson.

five playlists, five biomes, sequenced across the afternoon like five sides of one record. the music is mostly ambient, though it moves through many genres and countries, from the 1950s to this year. field recordings run through all of it.

the borders between them are soft. if the air felt different from one to the next, that was the intention.
