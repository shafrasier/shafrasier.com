The Listening Room

A new environment for discovery, connection, and interdisciplinary experience.

Scroll

Welcome to the Listening Room at Fugue Gallery. At its center is a 6.1 spatial audio system designed and hand-built by Otis Studio; around it hang more than fifteen artworks and design objects created by artists from Brooklyn to Berlin.

Come engage with transformative art among other curious and open individuals. We hope you leave grounded and inspired.

The room runs quiet and unhurried. Come and go anytime. Spend an afternoon with a carefully sequenced program of music in an intimate room. No reservation required, though reserving ahead helps us hold space for you.

We pour chilled traditional teas and sparkling water. Outlets and Wi-Fi are here; books, journaling, and small crafts are welcome. To preserve the listening environment, we ask you to leave calls, meetings, and anything that pulls you out of it at the door.

Daylight listening runs every Sunday in July and August, 11am–4pm. Come alone or with company.

✱

These sessions are for those who want to —

  • hear music in a human way, past the algorithm
  • meet people, make friends
  • tune out the noise of Manhattan
  • sit with new sounds and arrive at their own meaning

The room runs calm and unhurried — come ready to give the music your attention, and others theirs.

✱

A few things we ask —

  • Please, no shoes inside the listening room.
  • Keep your voice low, so everyone can sink into the music.
  • Bring a book, a sketchbook, a film camera — whatever lets you settle in.
  • Parties of four or fewer; for a larger group, please book a consultation.
  • Questions? Please contact us.
