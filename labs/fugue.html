welcome to the Clearing at Fugue Gallery.

the Clearing provides space for pure sound as collective ceremony. It also foregrounds discovery, interdisciplinary experience, and human connection.

at its center is a 6.1 spatial audio system designed and hand-built by Otis Studio. Around it hang more than fifteen art and design objects created by artists from Brooklyn to Berlin.

the Clearing runs quiet and unhurried. It is a vessel for carefully sequenced programs of music, and for the hours to fall into them.

come and go anytime.

we hope you leave grounded, inspired, and present to the world.

We pour chilled traditional teas and sparkling water. Outlets and Wi-Fi are here; books, journaling, and small crafts are welcome. To preserve the listening environment, we ask you to leave calls, meetings, and anything that pulls you out of it at the door.

These sessions are for those who want to

  • hear music in a human way, past the algorithm
  • meet people, make friends
  • tune out the noise of Manhattan
  • sit with new sounds and arrive at their own meaning

We ask that you

  • remove your shoes upon entering the Clearing.
  • keep your voice low, so everyone can sink into the music.
  • bring a book, a sketchbook, a journal — whatever lets you settle in.
  • come in parties of four or fewer. For a larger group, please book a consultation.
  • please contact us with any questions.
