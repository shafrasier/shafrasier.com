The Clearingat Fugue Gallery

A new environment for discovery, connection, and interdisciplinary experience.

Welcome to The Clearing at Fugue Gallery. At its center is a 6.1 spatial audio system designed and hand-built by Otis Studio; around it hang more than fifteen artworks and design objects created by artists from Brooklyn to Berlin.

Come engage with transformative art among other curious and open individuals. We hope you leave grounded and inspired.

Spend an afternoon with a carefully sequenced program of music in an intimate room. Come alone or with company. The room runs quiet and unhurried, so come and go anytime. No reservation required, though reserving ahead helps us hold space for you.

We pour chilled traditional teas and sparkling water. Outlets and Wi-Fi are here; books, journaling, and small crafts are welcome. To preserve the listening environment, we ask you to leave calls, meetings, and anything that pulls you out of it at the door.

These sessions are for those who want to

  • hear music in a human way, past the algorithm
  • meet people, make friends
  • tune out the noise of Manhattan
  • sit with new sounds and arrive at their own meaning

We ask that you

  • remove your shoes upon entering the clearing.
  • keep your voice low, so everyone can sink into the music.
  • bring a book, a sketchbook, a journal — whatever lets you settle in.
  • come in parties of four or fewer. For a larger group, please book a consultation.
  • please contact us with any questions.

Mission

The Clearing exists to provide a physical space for the collective ceremony of pure sound.

Vision

We imagine a city with room to listen: gatherings where strangers settle around sound, and everyone leaves more present to the music, each other, and themselves.
