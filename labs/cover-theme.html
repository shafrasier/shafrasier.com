Sample two colours straight off the artwork to override an album's auto-generated theme. Pick a light-background colour and an accent colour (the SIDE ONE headers, links, and rules); the MAP lightens them into the page exactly as the preview shows. Drop a cover, click to sample into the active slot, then paste the frontmatter into the album note. Leave a slot blank to keep that half auto-sampled.
