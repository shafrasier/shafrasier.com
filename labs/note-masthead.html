Note masthead — every note type

The album / single / track notes already open with a designed masthead (artist · fact grid · genre pills · links). Here is that same card adapted to every other note type, using each type's real metadata. The card stays recognisably one design but leads with the right field per type, turns date/place lines into facts, genres / roles / people into pills, and the external links into a footer. A sandbox — nothing here is live yet; it shows how #53 would extend.

Jump to a type

How it adapts: the lead changes per type — a Genre/Scene shows its italic lineage breadcrumb, an Era shows its defining tension, a Moment shows its before→after, a theory Idea shows its plain-language gloss. Facts (dates, places) fall into the grid; genres / influences become green pills (filled when the genre has its own note), roles / people / institutions become accent pills. The links footer handles the streaming row, a lone RYM, or a Wikipedia-led row. A plain Idea with no metadata degrades gracefully to just its title + type.
